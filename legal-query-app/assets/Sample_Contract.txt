AGREEMENT OF SERVICE
This Agreement of Service ("Agreement") is entered into on May 30, 2025, by and between:

Party B agrees to provide consulting services to Party A, including strategic planning and market analysis, for a period of six (6) months commencing on June 1, 2025.

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware.
